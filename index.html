About me

Publicado por Christian 18 de Octubre, 2025

I started working after I finish high school at TecMilenio, my first job was at Teleperformance, that was a call center right next to my school so it was really close to me.

The reason I started working was to start gaining experience, practice my english with customer from the US and start getting money to buy things that I wanted at that time.

After working at Teleperformance for a while, I decided to look for new opportunities to grow professionally. I found a job at a tech company where I could use my English skills more effectively and work on interesting projects.

Career changing

While the pandemic hit, I was working from home office thorugh all the 2 years, thats were I started linking more the computer, being at my room, self studing and watching tutorials.

I decided to make a career change and start learning programming. I began taking online courses and practicing coding in my free time. I liked the opportunities, the options to always keep learning and the good salaries that the tech industry offers.

Academlo bootcamp

To accelerate my learning, I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp. Academlo, where I learned the fundamentals of web development and built several projects.

I quickly fell in love with the results, and being the creator of my own projects.
